## Overview

Chambers of Xeric (CoX) is the first raid in OSRS, located on the summit of Mount Quidamortem on Zeah. It scales based on the number of players and their combat levels.

## Recommended Stats

- 90+ in all combat stats (melee, ranged, magic)
- 78+ Herblore (for making potions inside the raid)
- 70+ Mining, Woodcutting, Farming for resource rooms
- Prayer: 77+ for Rigour/Augury is a huge boost

## Room Strategies

### Tekton
- Switch to melee for this room
- Attack during his animation, run away when he goes to the anvil
- 4:0 method is optimal for experienced players

### Vasa Nistirio
- Use ranged to attack the crystals
- Kill crystals quickly to prevent healing
- Stay spread out to avoid AoE damage

### Olm (Final Boss)
- Head phase: Mage hand attacks, dodge fire walls
- Melee hand: Run the "4:1" or "3:1" method
- Final phase: Dodge crystals, pray correctly, DPS the head
